Before You Join
Be Specific

Name the process, the machine, the standard. Vague posts attract vague replies and teach nobody anything.

Be Verifiable

If you have credentials, say so. The verified-expert badge exists so others know how much weight to give your answer.

No Promotion

Vendor promotion, affiliate links and self-serving posts are removed. This is a professional knowledge platform, not a marketplace.
